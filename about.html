Experience

Mr. Brigmon has over 6 years of professional planning, Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Community Development, and Data Analytic experience. He has extensive experience in GIS, 3D modeling, web application development, and land use analysis. His professional accomplishments include: receiving Honorable Mention at the EPA’s P3 Design Competition for Sustainability; presenting his urban rail analysis in Austin, TX at the ESRI GeoDesign Summit in January 2015; and serving as Dean’s Ambassador for the UT School of Architecture. Mr. Brigmon’s experience as representative and analyst give him a range of skills and expertise on which to draw. He holds a BS in Business Administration from the College of Charleston and an MS in Community and Regional Planning from the University of Texas at Austin.
